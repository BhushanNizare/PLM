Overview

Polarion ALM is a complete, cloud-based agile software development management system that encompasses requirements management, agile project management, and Micro Focus' market-leading Quality Center test management tools. ProdigyPLM helps provide seamless traceability from requirements through project management to testing. In short, Polarion ALM serves as a comprehensive repository for all elements created during the creation of new software.
